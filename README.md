🧬 About Me
I hold a B.Eng in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering (Second Class Upper Honours) from the University of Ilorin, Nigeria, where I was supervised by Professor I. I. Ahmed.

Christian Onyeoziri — where I explore innovative solutions and sustainable manufacturing techniques at the intersection of materials science and artificial intelligence.
